Beck Depression Inventory (BDI-II)

This local self-report inventory consists of 20 items. Each item has four statements; pick the one that best describes how you have felt over the past two weeks, including today. Your answers are saved as a JSON file on your computer in the results/ folder next to this app.

Note: question 9 (suicidal ideas) is omitted in this version. Severity ranges have been scaled to a 0–60 maximum accordingly.
